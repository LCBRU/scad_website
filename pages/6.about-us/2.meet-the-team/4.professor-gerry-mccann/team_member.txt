text: ## Lead for SCAD Imaging

Prof McCann is a Consultant Cardiologist at Leicester Glenfield Hospital and a specialist in cardiovascular imaging. He is nationally recognised for his expertise in Cardiovascular Magnetic Resonance Imaging and is particularly interested in the study of in-vivo cardiac disease using novel imaging techinques.
