-

text_main: SCAD symptoms are those of a heart attack.

These include:

- Chest Pain
- Pain going into your arms, shoulder or jaw.
- Difficulty with breathing
- Nausea
- Dizziness
- Sweating
- Palpitations - the sensation of feeling your heart beating fast

If these symptoms are experienced, please seek urgent medical attention.
