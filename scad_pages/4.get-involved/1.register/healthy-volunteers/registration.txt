text: If you have not suffered a SCAD personally, you may still be able to get involved!
We are currently looking for **healthy volunteers**,
so as to provide controls for comparison with our SCAD patients.
Please note, if you are **directly (genetically) related to a SCAD survivor you would not be eligible**
to be a healthy volunteer, because some of our studies involve genetics. You could however register as a
[family member](/get-involved/register/family-and-relatives). If you are the friend or partner of a SCAD
survivor however you can register as a healthy volunteer.

<div class="alert alert-warning" role="alert">
  No clinical history please
</div>
