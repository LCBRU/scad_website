text: If you have had a Spontaneous Coronary Artery Dissection, and you'd be interested in helping us with some of the research we are doing into this rare and little-understood condtion, we are very keen to hear from you. Please remember that filling in this form DOES NOT mean you have commited to any research projects - it is only to find out more information.
